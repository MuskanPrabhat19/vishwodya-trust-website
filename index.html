About Vishwodya

Vishwodya Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering digital empowerment across India. We believe in harnessing technology to create positive social change, with a focus on education, health, and self-reliance.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide and ensure that every individual, regardless of their background, has access to the tools and knowledge needed to thrive in the modern world. We are passionate about serving our community and building a digitally-empowered Bharat.

Our Commitment

Vishwodya Trust is officially registered and committed to transparency and accountability. We are dedicated to our mission of service and digital development. All our initiatives are designed to have a tangible and positive impact on society.
